Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

This privacy policy governs your use of the software applications ("Applications") for mobile devices that were created by Lady and the Cats. This includes, but is not limited to, Cats! and Magical Lady's Tarot.

1. What information do the Applications obtain?

User Provided Information

The Applications generally store game data (such as high scores, card readings, or save files) locally on your device. For certain features (like leaderboards), data may be synced to a secure cloud database (e.g., Google Firebase) to preserve your progress.

Automatically Collected Information

The Applications may collect certain information automatically, including, but not limited to, the type of mobile device you use, your mobile operating system, and information about the way you use the Application (e.g., game scores and crash reports).

2. Does the Application collect precise real-time location?

No. Our Applications do not collect precise information about the location of your mobile device.

3. Third Parties

We may work with analytics and advertising partners (like Google AdMob) who need to know how you interact with the Application to display advertisements. We do not share your personal identity with these parties.

4. Children

We do not use the Application to knowingly solicit data from or market to children under the age of 13. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that his or her child has provided us with information without their consent, please contact us.

5. Contact Me

If you have any questions regarding privacy while using the Application, or have questions about our practices, please contact me via email at ladyandthecats550@gmail.com.
