About the Creator

Hi! I'm Rhea (also known as rheahime on Wattpad). I am an indie developer and writer based in the Philippines.

My studio, Lady and the Cats, is inspired by my tribe of 28 rescue cats and my dog, Sansa. Whether I'm coding a new game feature or drafting a new chapter, my goal is to create fun experiences and heartwarming stories that bring a little bit of magic to your day.
